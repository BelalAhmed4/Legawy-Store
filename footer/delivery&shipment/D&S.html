Delivery & Shipment

- We deliver to all areas across Egypt.

After successfully placing your order, our customer service will conduct a verification process by call/WhatsApp, then you can expect to receive your order within 3 to 5 business days.

- Shipment Fees

Our shipment fees vary from a province to another, therefore please check the shipping fees before confirming your order.

- Delivery Options

  • Home Delivery

    Orders are shipped Saturday to Thursday every week of the year.

  • Click & Collect

    After placing your order, you’ll receive a confirmation call/Whatsapp message with order number. Please wait for an email and/or call from us telling you when it’s ready to collect.

    We keep your order in store for 7 business days.

    If you encounter any issues with the product, such as breakage or defects, please don't hesitate to contact us. We will gladly facilitate the return process, ensuring you receive a full refund promptly.
